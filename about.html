SmartLink’s Journey Toward Digital Leadership

Smart Link began in 2004 with advanced solutions for contact centers and business process outsourcing in Saudi Arabia. As market needs grew, we developed a comprehensive ecosystem of communication and customer experience solutions, powered by technologies like AI, robotic process automation, ERP, and Microsoft Dynamics 365 for digital transformation.

Our operations expanded across the Kingdom, with international centers strengthening our regional presence. Since 2005, we have supported hundreds of government and private sector entities, earning a spot among Forbes' Top 100 Gulf Companies and becoming a leader in contact centers and customer experience in the region.
