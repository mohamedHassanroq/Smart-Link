Structured Protection Based on Globally Recognized Frameworks and Standards

At Smart Link, we implement internationally aligned cybersecurity standards that ensure comprehensive protection, effective risk management, and secure business continuity without disrupting daily operations.

ISO/IEC 27001

An international framework for information security management that focuses on data protection, risk management, and the implementation of clear security policies to ensure business continuity and reduce the likelihood of breaches.

NCA

National standards aimed at enhancing organizations’ security readiness through structured governance, asset management, and the protection of systems and digital infrastructure.

GDPR

A regulatory framework focused on protecting personal data, ensuring privacy, and regulating how data is collected, processed, and stored in a way that reduces legal risks.
